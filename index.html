Keep Tech Conferences about Tech

The following list of signatories agree that the type of debate they are interested in partaking in with respect to a tech conference is that which involves tech.

We believe that Politics, Religion, Social and other issues that are not related to tech are best left to other platforms of debate.

Signatories:

  • Craig Tataryn (@craiger) - Spaces - April 11, 2016

Where this originated:
